Shimane Shrines

Shimane prefecture is home to one of the most important shrines in Japan and one of the oldest. Some people believe it is the oldest shrine in the country. Once upon a time, part of the shrine was constructed on huge wooden pillars with a staircase leading towards it. It was the largest wooden structure in Japan. In October gods from everywhere in Japan come here to meet and discuss the coming year.
